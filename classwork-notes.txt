Week 5:

To try out web fonts that you host from your subdomain, check out this short tutorial at CSS Tricks: https://css-tricks.com/snippets/css/using-font-faceLinks to an external site.. I've uploaded four weights of Futura LT, in WOFF2 and WOFF, for you to experiment with.

Also try out Google Fonts, and explore subsets to reduce the page weight.

In a breakout room, work on your home page, or a new page, applying type in weights and styles. Also, use grid to make a different layout for larger screens, using a media query with a breakpoint that targets larger screens. Give and get help from your peers in the breakout room.

Have fun, and make sure to post a description of what you did and challenges you encountered.
