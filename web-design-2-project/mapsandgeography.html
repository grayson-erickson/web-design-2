Major regions and countries of Verdenzye
Region Location Description
Genesye Central continent The origin continent for the sapient residents of the planet, airdropped by an ancient spacefaring culture and revered as the cultural and scientific center for all known countries.
Omelye Southern continent Known for fishing and farms, with a central lake full of boat-cities and a rural culture dominated by established farming families.
Koury Northern peninsula More mountainous and industrialized than Omelye, with its own boat-cities and a recent break toward full industrialization.
Shune Tropical southeast A glamorous tourist destination with hidden crime and grey-market culture beneath its resort surface.
Roko Western highlands A harsh, mountainous island continent whose poor nations still wield power through access to Abyssal mysteries.
The Meritomes Island archipelago An isolationist set of islands focused on tourism, local crafts, and living outside the major settled continents.
Yumior Swamp continent A swampy land of harsh terrain, strange religions, and ancient mysteries.
Dendae Split continent Divided into East Dendae's constitutional queendoms and West Dendae's orderly nations, with ongoing political tension between them.
Caeryn Coastal frontier A swampy coastal frontier settled by refugees and extremists, organized as a loose confederation rather than formal countries.
Zerif Western frontier A newly discovered continent rich in exotic ores, barely settled along its western coast, and rumored to hide a massive labyrinth.
Yrrim Northern tundra A cold, distant continent covered in reddish grass and barely settled beyond a few southern ports.
The Abyss Oceanic sinkhole A massive sea-floor hole that emits strange weather and supplies exotic items and creatures to collectors and leaders.
Trane Southern desert A southern nitrogen desert still mostly explored by expedition teams and frontier foundations.
