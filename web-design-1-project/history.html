History of my world

A (currently brief) history of Lilians (half-humans) living on Verdenzye:

0-4000: Lilians landed on Genesye and settled in. Most of this time was spent getting used to the new environment and understanding the technology of their creators.

4001-10006: Many technological revolutions happened around this time period, as well as a fair bit of expansion.

10007-present (25044): Curiosity runs rampant among the entire population, and the desire for adventure is at its highest point. The world map is rapidly expanding, and many discoveries are being made! The current map of Verdenzye is about the size of earth's map, but the perimeter of Verdenzye itself is the size of at least 8 of those maps! There are also rumors of a whole world deep underground....
