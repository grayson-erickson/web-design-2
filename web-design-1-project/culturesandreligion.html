Cultures and faiths of my world

For the project, I'm mostly gonna go into the religions and cults of this world. I'll expand this and add other cultural stuff in the future!

  • Sun Worshippers (major faith)
  • Muse Adherents (regional to Yumior)
  • Only known religious cult

Sun Worshippers

Faith Beliefs
Sun Worshippers
  • Believe their sun, Sofyel, is a god who created the world.
  • Occasionally worship 5 moon goddesses.
  • Give thanks to the sun daily.
  • Myths involve the Dark Sun, either as an evil figure or another side of Sofyel.

Muse Control (regional to Yumior)

Faith Beliefs
Muse Control
  • Ancestral religion centered on a myth of 13 Muses and their leader, Cysa.
  • Belief that creativity is powerful and must be controlled or tricked.
  • Focuses on creative magic more than worship.
  • Includes schisms, including one that treats Cysa as a feminine power figure.
  • References are common in media, but true belief is strongest in Yumior.

The only known religious cult

Faith Beliefs
Siluet Cult
  • Most cults in Verdenzye are drug cults or shady businesses.
  • This cult is a religious offshoot of Muse Control.
  • They believe the 14th Muse is chained in Zerif's giant labyrinth.
  • Rituals involve suffering in exchange for boons.
